Common questions.

What's included in the free audit?

We'll run 10 targeted queries about your business on ChatGPT and Perplexity, score your visibility against your local competitors, and send you a full report — at no cost. No sales pitch until you've seen the data.

How is this different from SEO?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the discipline of making your business the answer AI gives. It requires a fundamentally different approach than keyword rankings — one that most agencies don't yet offer.

Do you need access to my website or accounts?

No. We run the audit using publicly available information. The Starter Fix deliverables are yours to implement yourself — or hand to your developer.

What kinds of businesses benefit most?

Service businesses with local customers — dentists, physical therapists, chiropractors, lawyers, contractors, salons, restaurants, and any brick-and-mortar business that relies on local reputation and word-of-mouth.
